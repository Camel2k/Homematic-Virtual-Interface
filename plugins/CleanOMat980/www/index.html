Roomba980 Plugin

This plugin generates a 4 Key remote control at your CCU. The device is named Cleo980
First you have to setup the access credentials for your 980 in plugin settings.
You may then control your robot thru the remote.


Channel 1 : Short Keypress = Start your robot
Channel 2 : Short Keypress = Stop your robot
Channel 3 : Short Keypress = Dock your robot

Channel 4 contains 3 status fields:
CUR_STATE = current status of your robot
BATTERIE = level of the robot battery
BIN is true when you have to empty the robots dust bin.
